FAQ

Q:

What to do if my controller is damaged?

A:

If your controller is still under warranty, it is recommended to take it back to its location of purchase along with any proof of purchase for a refund/replacement. If there is no warranty or it has expired, it is recommended to take the controller to a business that specializes in technology repair for a diagnosis.

Q:

What do the lights on the controller mean?

A:

The DualSense Wireless Controller has two light systems:

Light Bar (beside the touchpad):

  • Soild white: controller is on
  • Soild orange: rest mode
  • Flashing blue/white: connection or boot error

Player Indicator (below the touchpad):

  • Shows how many controllers are connected
  • 1 light = first controller, 2 lights = second, etc.
  • The first controller has priority for controlling the console
